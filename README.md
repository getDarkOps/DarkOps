## What It Does

Most creators and brands see a trend after it peaks. By the time they script, shoot, edit, and post — the moment is gone. AlphaCMO eliminates that gap entirely.

**Every night at 2AM**, the engine scans thousands of content signals across your niche. By morning, you have a report of what's trending right now — with viral scores, engagement data, and ready-to-use hooks.

**Say "go" from your phone.** The engine rewrites trending scripts in your brand voice, generates professional avatar videos, applies branded overlays, and delivers finished content — ready to post. The entire pipeline runs autonomously. You approve. It publishes.

**The result:** Clients consistently capture trends 12-48 hours before competitors, contributing to tens of thousands of additional views per video. Content that would take a team days to produce is delivered in hours.

## How It Works

```
Trend Detection → Script Adaptation → Video Production → Brand Overlay → Publishing
```

- **Scout** — Continuously monitors trending content signals, filters for high-potential opportunities, and scores them by viral trajectory
- **Creator** — Adapts trending scripts to match your brand voice, generates avatar videos, and composites branded overlays with captions
- **Distribute** — Publishes finished content to social platforms on your schedule

Each client runs on a dedicated, isolated instance. Nothing is shared. Your brand voice, your hashtags, your content — completely private.
