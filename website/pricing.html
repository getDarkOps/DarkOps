Questions

What is an OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is the AI agent platform that powers DarkOps. When you sign up, we deploy your own dedicated OpenClaw instance on a private server — 10 specialized agents running exclusively for your brand. It's your machine. We set it up and maintain it.

What exactly does DarkOps do?

Every night, your 10 agents scan Instagram for viral talking-head reels in your niche, transcribe and score them, rewrite the best scripts in your brand voice, generate AI avatar videos with branded overlays, and publish to your Instagram. You control everything from Telegram on your phone.

Do I need to be on camera?

No. DarkOps generates avatar videos using AI. You can use a stock avatar, upload a photo to create a custom one, or use your own footage. Zero cameras required.

How much of my time does this take?

About 5 minutes per day. Review scripts and approve videos in Telegram. Say "promote" and your agents handle the rest.

Will this actually get me more views?

DarkOps doesn't guess at content. It finds what's already going viral in your niche, adapts proven hook structures for your brand, and publishes consistently. More content from proven patterns means more views on your offers — daily.

Is my server shared with anyone else?

No. Every customer gets their own dedicated DigitalOcean Droplet VPS. Your brand voice, your data, your agents. Fully isolated.

How is this different from other AI marketing tools?

Most AI marketing tools output strategy docs and text. DarkOps outputs finished videos — published to your Instagram. You get your own server running 10 agents, not a shared SaaS login. Different category entirely.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no setup fees. Cancel from your billing dashboard and your server shuts down at the end of your billing period.
