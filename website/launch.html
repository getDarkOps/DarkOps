Questions

What exactly does AlphaCMO do?

Every night, AlphaCMO scans Instagram for viral talking-head reels in your niche, transcribes and scores them, rewrites the best scripts in your brand voice, generates AI avatar videos with branded overlays, and publishes to your Instagram. You control everything from Telegram on your phone.

Do I need to be on camera?

No. AlphaCMO generates avatar videos using AI. You can use a stock avatar, upload a photo to create a custom one, or use your own footage. Zero cameras required.

How much of my time does this take?

About 5 minutes per day. Review scripts and approve videos in Telegram. That's it.

Is the content actually original?

Yes. AlphaCMO discovers proven viral hook structures and rewrites them entirely for your voice, audience, and CTA. Only the hook pattern is borrowed — the content is 100% original.

What platforms does it publish to?

Instagram Reels at launch. TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Facebook Reels are on the roadmap.

How is this different from Okara or other AI marketing tools?

Most AI marketing tools output strategy docs and text content. AlphaCMO outputs finished videos — published to your Instagram. Trend discovery through publishing, fully automated. Different category entirely.
